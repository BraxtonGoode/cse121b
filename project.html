W06-7: Project

Mars Rover Photos

This Page is meant to show all the photos taken from the Spirit and Curiosity rover on Mars.
All you have to do is type a number between 1 through 1000 and select a rover from the
drop down menu and then click on Apply Filter. There are days that do not contain photos
for example day 500 of the Curiosity rover and when you apply it it will say down below
the button "No photos available for the selected day."
